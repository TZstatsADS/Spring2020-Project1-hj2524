Introduction

The musics have played an important in our daily life. In this project, we applied texting mining techniques to explore what artists expressed in their songs, what kind of sentiments they wanted to show based on the lyrics.

Is Pop Music more positive?

In this part, I would like to find the most frequently used words in lyrics of Pop music. From the word cloud below, it shows that “love” is the most frequently word. It is reasonable because when people talk about musics, the first word that came to her mind might be “love”.

Moreover, the below plots show the top 10 frequently used positive and negative words in lyrics and their frequencies. Those positive words, like “love” “kiss” , seem to be used more frequently than negative words, like “boy” “fall” “lie”. It might be consistent with our intuition that there are more musics about “love” or showed a positive sentiment.

Next, we plot the word cloud for Pop music again. But we take the sentiment into account and separate the positive and negative words. Same as before, “love” is the most frequently used word. And we cannot conclude there are more positive word or not.

pop_plot_sentiment%>%
    acast(term ~ sentiment, value.var = "n", fill = 0) %>%
    comparison.cloud(colors = c("gray20", "gray80"),
                     max.words = 150)
